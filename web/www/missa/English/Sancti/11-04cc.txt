[Oratio]
!For Ss. Vitalis and Agrícola Martyrs
Grant, almighty God, we beseech You, that we who keep the feast of Your blessed Martyrs, Vitalis and Agricola, may be helped by their intercession with You.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta]
!For Ss. Vitalis and Agrícola Martyrs
Be appeased, O Lord, we beseech You, by the gifts we offer, and by the intercession of Your holy Martyrs, Vitalis and Agricola, protect us from all dangers.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
!For Ss. Vitalis and Agrícola Martyrs
May this Communion, O Lord, cleanse us of sin and make us partakers of heavenly healing.
$Per Dominum
