[Oratio]
!Commemoration of St. Felix, Priest and Martyr
Grant, we beseech You, almighty God, that the example of Your saints may urge us on to a better life, so that we may imitate the deeds of those whose feasts we celebrate.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta]
!Commemoration of St. Felix, Priest and Martyr
Graciously accept, O Lord, the sacrifices we offer You in honor of Your blessed Martyr Felix, and grant that they may bring us everlasting help.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
!Commemoration of St. Felix, Priest and Martyr
Refreshed by the sacrament of salvation, we beseech You, O Lord, that we may be helped by the prayers of blessed Felix, Your Martyr, whose feast we are celebrating.
$Per Dominum
