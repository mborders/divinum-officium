[Secreta]
Graciously accept the sacrificial gifts offered You, O Lord, through the merits of blessed Margaret, Your Virgin and Martyr, and grant that they may prove an unfailing aid for us.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
We who have been refreshed by the richness of Your divine sacrament, beseech You, O Lord, our God, that through the intercession of blessed Margaret, Your Virgin and Martyr, we may forevermore abide in its participation.
$Per Dominum
