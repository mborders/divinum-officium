St. Thecla

[Oratio]
!For St. Thecla
Grant, we beseech You, O almighty God, that we who celebrate the anniversary of the death of blessed Thecla, Your Virgin and Martyr, may rejoice on her yearly feastday and benefit by the example of such great faith.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta]
!For St. Thecla
Accept, O Lord, the gifts we bring on the festival of blessed Thecla, Your Virgin and Martyr, through whose patronage we hope to be delivered.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
!For St. Thecla
May the sacrament we have received, aid us, O Lord, and by the intercession of blessed Thecla, Your Virgin and Martyr, may it keep us forevermore under Your everlasting protection.
$Per Dominum
