[Oratio_]
O God, Who made blessed John, Your Confessor and Teacher, an extraordinary lover of perfect self-denial and the Cross, grant that, by continually striving to imitate him, we may reach everlasting glory.
$Per Dominum

[Secreta]
May the loving prayer of blessed John, Your Confessor and Doctor, fail us never, O Lord; may it commend our offerings and ever secure for us Your forgiveness.
$Per eumdem

[Postcommunio]
So that your sacrificial rites may grant us salvation, we pray You, O Lord, that blessed John, Your Confessor and illustrious Doctor, may draw nigh as our intercessor.
$Per Dominum
