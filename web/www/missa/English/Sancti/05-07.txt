[Oratio]
O God, in defense of Whose honor Stanislaus, the glorious Bishop, died by the swords of wicked men, grant, we beseech You, that all who seek his help may obtain salvation as a result of his prayers.
$Per Dominum

[Postcommunio]
May this Communion, O Lord, cleanse us from guilt, and, through the intercession of Blessed Stanislaus, Your Martyr and Bishop, make us share in the heavenly remedy.
$Per Dominum
